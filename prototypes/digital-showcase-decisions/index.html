What each option trades

Option A

One true page; deep links land on the right section via #anchors; zero duplicated content; the seven routes stay live as a contract. Cost: redirect hop on sub-route entry.

Option B

No redirects; every route renders something. Cost: seven near-duplicate thin pages of the same single-page content — weaker SEO signals, more surface to keep honest, and the “single-page Starter” promise quietly stops being true.
