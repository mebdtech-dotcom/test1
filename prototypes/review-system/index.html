Verified engagement
28 Jun 2026

We engaged Padma for a complete boiler-feed-line valve package — 40 DN100 gate valves plus the associated flanged spools and fittings. From RFQ to delivery the process was genuinely professional. Their quotation was itemised against our datasheet line by line, and they raised two sensible technical clarifications on the trim material before quoting rather than after. Every unit shipped with hydro-test and mill certificates, and delivery landed two days inside the quoted window. The one bit of friction was a packaging dent on a single crate, which had no impact on function and which they proactively flagged on the delivery note. On the strength of this engagement we've added them to our preferred-supplier list for gate-valve work.
